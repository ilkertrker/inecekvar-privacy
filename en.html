Türkçe Wake Me There — Privacy Policy Last updated: July 2026 Location Data Wake Me There uses your location only while an alarm is active, so it can wake you when you approach your destination. This location data is processed on your device; it is not sent to any server or stored for the purpose of the alarm. Place Search When you search for a destination, the text you type and your approximate location are sent to the Google Places service to return results. This happens only while you are searching. For how Google handles this data, see the Google Privacy Policy. Data Stored on Your Device Your recent destinations are stored only on your device so you can reuse them with one tap. This data is not shared with us or any third party. We Don't Collect Data We do not create accounts, collect personal information, or use advertising or analytics trackers.
